About Us

Creating turnkey solutions & sustainability through valued partnerships & service with a difference. Established in 2009, it is managed by two of the main members of the business that have extensive logistics and courier services background & knowledge. We have experience in Road and Airfreight Industry. Our services is a combination of all above fitted into a courier concern. Our sound knowledge of the Republic of RSA and the ability to get anywhere in a reasonable time frame gives our customer the perfect solution for their shipments.

Our Goals

S.L.R Freight and Rea Pele Innovations is a tool that can not only benefit your freight movement needs but also benefit time management and profitability. We are commited to high service excellence and dependability. We have a compliment of our own fleet and various subcontractors to ensure highest level of freight distribution services. Our operations mirrors the hours of business of our client. Fast, dedicated and time critical demands are provided by our courier express delivery system. All shipments are monitored continuously by our operations department. The persuit of excellence is not an option for us but more of a business necessity. There is no more important goal for each individual than the achievement of consistently high standards of service.

Our differentiators include an expert operations and support team, exceptional drivers and a fleet of flatbed, tautliner, tail lift and Superlink trucks.
